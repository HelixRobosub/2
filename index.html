What is Robosub?

RoboSub is a global undewater robotics competition that chalenges team to face the difficutlies of underwater autnomous robotics. It was founded in 1997 and is held every year at U.S. Navy Space and Naval Warfare Systems Center Pacific's TRANSDEC Anechoic pool in San Diego, California.
